ABOUT ME

I am a mechanical and automation Engineer. My knowledge in the mechanical as well as automation field gives me a wider perspective and makes me ready for the ever-changing industries of CAD, CAM and CAE, and Engineering in general. Being alumni of a Premier government Engineering college has opened doors of exposure and opportunities making me ahead of my competition already.

My primary skills include modeling and analysis in various CAD, CAM Softwares in which I have won an inter-college competition, that too during the period of my early learning.

The fact that we can design, assemble, analyze, disassemble, and repeat without creating the component or system in the physical world excites me. Making me Dream in the day and keeping me awake in the night.

I am passionate about designing and building machine parts and systems and look to further improve my expertise in CAD and CAE, and engineering in general.

My inner drive for improvements and optimal performance is complemented by a sense of humility to stay focused on my goals. I live by the motto “learning should never stop” and am excited about what next there is to experience and learn. Being a gifted learner I tend to learn quickly.
